Project Overview

A prototype application designed in Figma and built in Unity for RMIT VEGA's Augmented Reality experience. This short-timeframe sprint focused on rapid prototyping, extensive user testing, and meeting a simulated industry brief under tight deadlines.

The VEGA AR-X project challenged me to work within industry-standard sprint methodologies while delivering a functional AR prototype that met specific user requirements and technical constraints.

Sprint Challenge

Deliver a working AR prototype within a 4-week sprint while conducting continuous user testing and iterative design improvements. The tight timeline required efficient prioritization and rapid decision-making.

Working with Unity for the first time in a UX context taught me valuable lessons about the intersection of design and development, particularly how technical constraints impact user experience decisions in emerging technologies like AR.
