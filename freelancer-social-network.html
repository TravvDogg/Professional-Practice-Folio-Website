Project Overview

Created a platform addressing real needs in the freelance community, helping freelancers find work and connect with clients. Supported by research with actual freelancers across multiple fields to find existing problems and propose solutions.

This project emphasized human-centered design by deeply understanding the freelance ecosystem, from the challenges freelancers face in finding consistent work to the difficulties clients experience in discovering quality talent.

Community-Driven Design

Conducted extensive interviews and surveys with freelancers across many fields to understand problems and the impact of proposed solutions.

The resulting platform prioritises authentic connections and meaningful project matching raditional bidding system to create a more usable and people first experience for both the freelancers and clients.
